These devices are found on ebay for about $0.50: here
There are little big, but extremely reliable. I've never had one that actually failed
We use 2 of them on our project. Both take the battery 7.4V as input.
One is used for the Camera 3.3V and passed from the chasis to the turret
One is used for the arduino-nano at 5v, but possibly 3.3V could work for that also
The only issue is that the turret motor uses 5V to drive but that could be obtained from the H-Bridge

Homework

Attach the input to at least 7 V or more it can handle 30V DC
Then use the screw adjust and a voltmeter to change the voltage output to 5v and 3.3V
