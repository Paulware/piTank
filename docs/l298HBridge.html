H-Bridge for Treads

Requirements

  • The HBridge shall be powered by a 7.4V (2S LIPO) or a 11.1 (3S LIPO)
  • The HBridge shall be controlled via 4 PWM outputs from the nano microcontroller
  • The HBridge shall be located in the rear of the chasis between the 2 gears

Discussion

When an 11.1V battery powers this device, a pin-jumper should be removed

Batt - => GND
